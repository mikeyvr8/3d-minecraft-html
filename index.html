Mini Minecraft — Offline Isometric 3D

   
Single HTML file. Double-click to open (works from file://). Drag to pan, scroll to zoom, left-click place, right-click remove, 1–6 change block.
   
     
Block palette (1-6)
     
   
   
                           
   
               
   
   
   
Rendering: isometric voxel engine (2.5D). Fast on Chromebooks and offline.
